## The hypothesis

Some strategies are _the same alpha_ across regimes; others are not. Scaling exposure by an external regime indicator — a smoothed VIX percentile — is a textbook risk-management overlay.

## What the project does

- Base strategy: long-only crypto basket rebalanced weekly.
- Regime indicator: smoothed VIX percentile rank (10-day EMA of percentile).
- Overlay: scale exposure inversely to the regime indicator.
- Compare Sharpe / max DD with and without the overlay.

The headline Sharpe barely moves. The equity curve shape changes materially: 16 percentage points of drawdown absorbed by a regime-aware size scaler.

## What's transferable

Risk overlays don't show up in headline alpha. They show up in the worst-year column of a fund's tear sheet. This is the discipline of _risk-managed return_, not raw return.
