## School Principal role

STA Philippines (Tier-1) is the local affiliate of the international Society of Technical Analysts body. In Apr 2026 the Tier-1 program held its graduation for the **CryptoneedsChrist University** cohort; I co-signed all 12 graduate certificates as School Principal.

The role sits on top of my own CTA cert (Dec 2025) and is unpaid, voluntary, and a vantage point on what Tier-1 finance education actually teaches — and what it leaves out.

## Curriculum feedback loop

The gaps I see in Tier-1 finance training map cleanly onto the eval-first method I now ship in the AI lane:

- **Multiple-testing discipline** is taught as a Sharpe caveat, not as a 31-gate harness.
- **Backtesting rigor** is taught as a curve-fit story, not as a frozen-spec + walk-forward contract.
- **Evaluation** of a strategy is taught as a Sharpe number, not as a deflated Sharpe + CSCV-PBO + MinBTL battery.

Surfacing those gaps back into the curriculum is a 12-month project, not a one-time memo.
