Binary market implies 31¢ for PH real GDP growth above 6.5% in 2026.
My macro-overlay model places fair probability at 36¢. The drivers: (1)
services-export growth has now run at > 10% YoY for eight consecutive
quarters, the longest such run since the 2014-2016 BPO boom; (2) the
BSP has 75bp of cumulative cuts over the cycle, the bulk of which
have a 6-12 month transmission lag; (3) the negative drag from net
exports is fading as the electronics cycle bottoms. The edge is small
but the falsifier is clean: two weak quarterly GDP prints (sub-5.5%)
between now and Q3 2026 closes the trade.
